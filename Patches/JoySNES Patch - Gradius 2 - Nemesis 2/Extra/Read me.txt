Gradius 2 - Nemesis 2 (FR).ips file is a patch to translate to French.

Gradius 2 - Nemesis 2 [FRS].ips file is a patch made by FRS to enhance
the game. (Smooth scroll on MSX2+/turbo R, Dynamic-Vsync, etc)

Gradius 2 Kittiya Ko (KR).ips is a patch to translate to Korean.

Gradius 2 - Nemesis 2 (PT).ips a patch to translate to Portugese.

The patch for JoySNES in not included. You can apply a JoySNES patch before
or after one of these patches if you use the corresponding patch.
